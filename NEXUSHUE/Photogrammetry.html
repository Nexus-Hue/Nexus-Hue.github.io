    Photogrammetry
    • 3D Scans
    • Feb 27 2022

    During winter break I learned about photogrammetry and have been wanting to use it for videos. As of right now, I have only scanned small objects like hats, shoes, and an Mr2. I plan to scan locations to bring into unreal engine 5 for hyper-realistic scenes. The only problem is that scanning stuff requires very even lighting. The best time to go out and scan stuff is when it’s foggy…but not too foggy. You basically just don’t want any harsh shadows because they will get baked into the model. Although you can still make it work if you retexture the model. It works best for statues because you could easily find another texture to replace the material and it would not look out of place.
