    Marooned, the first mvp
    • 3D Animation
    • March 11, 2022

    This video is my first public prototype. The video did much better than I thought it would on Instagram. Especially for being more of a tech demo than an actual product. It managed to reach just under 5k views. I know it isn’t that much but still not bad considering the account is just starting. According to the insights, account reach went up by 98,380% which sounds pretty good but it’s probably just because I don’t post often. So, I’m not sure what to make of it if I’m being honest lol.

    I also have no idea if anyone who saw it would be interested in being part of one of those animations. Although 17 people liked it enough to save it with a bookmark which I thought was cool. In contrast, the video did poorly on YouTube, getting only 20 views. I guess that it could be due to the copyright strike. The song I used was available to use for YouTube shorts, but I used the instrumental version which might have been the reason for the copyright strike. So, in conclusion, YouTube might work better for tutorials since I know many people, including myself. Use YouTube as their main source for tutorials. Although I will probably still try uploading another similar animation as a Youtube short to be sure. It did okay on TikTok, but I was expecting it to do the best there since the app is music-related. It got around 200 views but in comparison to Instagram, it didn’t do so well. Although I also only have 2 followers on there. My Instagram reel might have done better thanks to the people who shared it and got others to see it and share it.
