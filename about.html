WHY ADVERTISE ON YOUTUBE?

Unlike other platforms, this traffic is real. Now that's not to say they don't have a bot problem. THEY DO, but so does the rest of the internet. The key difference here is it's easier to build up an audience that actually gives a fuck about you.

Most socials are too fast passed and rely on trends. If you don't participate you risk getting ignored by the algorithms.

This makes it hard to build a real connection to the creator, because even if you get a ton of views. Using trends makes you blend in with all the other people doing trends.

It's a platform made by Google, so YouTube videos rank well in search engines. Also most phones come with google as the default search engine and Youtube usually comes pre-installed.

Strong foundation for conversions because of their targeting & analytics.

Target users based on:
★ search history ★ demographics ★ interests ★ behavior ★

It lets you to show ads to people who have previously interacted with your website or videos. Making it more likely they will buy.

Youtube is the only social media platform that has figured out the formula for paying creators. Giving you an additional revenue stream for your business.

Now I know you're probably thinking...hey I know other social media platforms that have a creator funds too tho...

& that's true, but Youtube is the only one that doesn't pay in peanuts.

Youtube has the best longevity out of ALL SOCIAL MEDIA. Not only have they withstood the test of time...time and time again, but your content has more chances to blow up.

Tiktok can give you "virality" one day and dump you the next day. Unlike other social media platforms where content has a shelf life Youtube still promotes old content if it becomes relevant. All other social media platforms have this issue. They only push your content for a while if at all, while Youtube on the other hand still gives your content the chance to blow up even years into the future.
