Neuro Releaf

Arthritis Cream

This project was made to practice how to design packaging for products. I also came up with the name. The Neuro half is in reference to arthritis and the releaf is a pun on relief and the CBD leaf. I decided to go with an arthritis cream because I myself have arthritis in my neck from a work injury.
