Why was this Kraken Ad made?

My first full Advertisement! There was a lot of things I wanted to test with this project. I wanted to get better at mixing music, so I started with that. I saw it as an opportunity to push my creative limits and experiment with combining multiples skills into one project. After I felt like I had something decent I started working on the video. I've made ads before, but this was the first time it really felt like I made something decent. Up until now my ads were just product promos & kept getting compared with social media posts, which I did not like. The same way Proximo Spirits aimed for â€œTHE NEW GOLD STANDARD in spiced rum thanks to its superb tasteâ€ I wanted my work to be seen as real advertisements and if I kept making videos like my coke promo I would have been boxed in... Music-Driven Storyboarding and Animation

The atmosphere was inspired by the music I mixed. The music was meant to be my own take on the soundtracks from Pirates of the Caribbean. Using choir samples to emphasize the biblical scale of the Kraken! I made a couple different versions of the song. They served as my storyboards for daydreaming about the scene while working on the animation. Some of these versions took very drastic turns. One version took the direction of going into the water and sinking along the shipwreck until you reach the bottom where all the gold and treasure is at.(That was the original location for the bottles)


Visual Design Reflecting Flavor and Brand

All the gold and treasure is there for the name of course. Since the story states that the rum gets it's taste form the Kraken's gold treasure getting infused with the drink.
